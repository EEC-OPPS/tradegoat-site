Questions owners actually ask

Is this really not per seat?

Really. Per-seat pricing punishes you for growing and makes you share logins to save money — the thing everybody hates about the big-name tools. TradeGOAT is flat per company within your tier's user count. Hiring your fourth tech costs you nothing.

What counts as a "user"?

A login. Owner, office, field tech — each person gets their own login with the right view for their role (techs see their work, not your money). Sharing logins is what we're trying to save you from.

What happens when my crew grows past my tier?

You move up one tier and everything comes with you — nothing to migrate, nothing re-set-up. Growth covers up to 10 people; past that, Pro is unlimited.

When do payments actually start?

When we exit the testing phase — and you'll know well in advance. We're building the boring parts properly first (the legal entity, the payment rails). Until then: pilots are free, the waitlist is real, and this page is the promise we'll be held to.

What about the AI agents I've heard about?

Add-on specialists (the first, a code-and-licensing reference agent, is already live for pilot companies) will price separately as an optional layer. Nothing about agents changes the core tiers above.
