Step 1 · Bring your own

Upload the proposal you already use. It becomes your template.

No starting over. TradeGOAT reads your existing Word or PDF proposal — your language, your terms, your warranty — and turns it into a reusable template where the customer name, address, and pricing fill themselves in.

  • Word in → editable Word out, ready to send
  • Your legal wording is never rewritten
  • One document becomes every future proposal

Step 2 · Bid it

Real costs in. One honest margin. Your price out.

Enter what the job actually costs — crew, days, parts, travel, even your overhead — and set one profit margin. The price is computed the way the math really works, so the profit you see is profit you keep.

  • Overhead is in the cost — no phantom profit
  • Per-service rates that follow your real pricing
  • Multiple options per proposal for the customer to choose

Step 5 · Run it in the field

Your paper service ticket — reborn on the tech's phone.

Upload the half-page ticket your techs fill out today (even a photo of it) and TradeGOAT rebuilds it as a digital form. Customer info fills itself, hours compute themselves (lunch deducted), photos attach, and the customer signs right on the glass.

  • Finalize & send — customer and office get the signed PDF instantly
  • Every ticket files itself to that unit's service history
  • Found a problem? Flag it — it feeds a repair proposal

Step 6 · Never miss a beat

Maintenance clocks, missed-visit watch, and a billing list that empties itself.

Every unit under contract carries a service clock. Finish a ticket and the clock resets; miss one and it's flagged before the customer notices. Billable work lands on the office's needs-billing list until the bill goes out — nothing slips.

  • Contract renewals surface before they lapse
  • One-page service history per unit, printable for the customer
  • "Got it running, didn't take payment" never gets forgotten

Step 7 · Learn from it

Know what the job really made — while it's still running.

Every job carries a scorecard: what you bid next to what it actually cost, category by category, with your true margin updating live. Tag the reason when a number slips — under-bid, delay, scope — and bid the next one sharper.

  • No more finding out at tax time
  • Owner sees dollars; the crew sees progress
  • Your bidding gets smarter with every job
