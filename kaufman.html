What it actually does

It starts from the paperwork you already have. Upload the proposal you've been sending for years and it becomes a reusable template — your format, your language, your payment terms. You don't rebuild your business inside someone else's app.
It prices work from your real costs. Crew, days, materials, overhead — one honest margin out. No phantom profit, and anyone in your shop can price a job the same way you would.
It tells you if the job is making money — while it's still running. Bid vs. actual by cost bucket, synced with QuickBooks, so you catch a job before it goes red instead of finding out at tax time.
It runs the field too. Time clock, tickets, and receipts on your techs' phones — the truck and the office on one system, in real time.
